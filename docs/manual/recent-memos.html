Recent Memos Feed

A single combined feed of job memos from every project, all in one place. Instead of opening each job to see what's new, you scan one list to catch up on activity across the whole shop.

Where it lives

Recent Memos is one of the folder tabs in the top-left of the portal, next to Project Portal. It's a portal-wide view, not a tab inside a single project — click it to leave the project list and see the cross-project feed.

What this view is for

Memos get written one project at a time, but sometimes you want the big picture: what's moved on every job lately? The Recent Memos Feed gathers those notes from all projects and surfaces the newest activity together, so you don't have to open jobs one by one to stay current.

What you'll see

Filter box
A search box labeled Filter memos by project, body, or author… — type to narrow the feed to just the memos you care about.
Match count
Shows how many memos match what you've typed, so you know how much the filter has narrowed things down.
Memo entries
Each memo shows which project it belongs to, the body of the note, and who wrote it.

How to use it

  1. Open the feed. Click the Recent Memos folder tab in the top-left of the portal.
  2. Filter to what you need. Type in the filter box to narrow by project name, by a word in the memo body, or by author. The match count updates as you type.
  3. Read across jobs. Scan the entries to see the latest notes from every project at once.
A fast morning catch-up

Open Recent Memos first thing in the day to see what happened across all active jobs without clicking into each one. Filter by your own name, or by a project number, to jump straight to what's relevant to you.

Editing memos

The memos here are the very same ones created in each project's Job Memos tab — this feed is just a cross-project window onto them. To add, change, or delete a memo, open the project it belongs to and use that project's Job Memos tab.
