Standard Operating Procedure Casting Wall Creation

Rev: 1.0
Cadence: Weekly — before the upcoming week
Owner: ____________
Print the 11×17 Board Schedule from the Weekly Schedule site and post it on the casting wall for the upcoming week. Confirm every casting is labeled before printing.
1

Verify all casting for the upcoming week is labeled Side A or Side B.

2

Check that the following Monday's casting is also labeled.

3

Go to the Weekly Schedule site.

4

Click the printer icon in the top-right of the screen.

5

In the print window that pops up, select Board Schedule (11×17) at the top.

6

Select the week you are printing in the drop-down.

7

If there is a Saturday casting, check the Include Saturday box.

8

Click Print Selected.

9

Scroll all the way to the bottom to check for any Unresolved Castings. Usually happens when a casting wasn't labeled — especially the following Monday.

10

Only if there are no unresolved castings, print to 11×17 paper, landscape.

11

Cut in half with scissors and discard the blank portion.

12

Remove last week's papers from the casting wall.

13

Use magnets to put up the casting schedule, aligning with the days of the week.

Bottom line: Confirm every casting is labeled, print the 11×17 Board Schedule, check for unresolved castings, then cut and post to the casting wall. Casting Wall Creation
