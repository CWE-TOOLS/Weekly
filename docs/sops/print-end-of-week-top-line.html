Standard Operating Procedure Print End-of-Week Top Line

Rev: 1.0
Cadence: Weekly — end of week
Owner: ____________
Do this after clearing the previous week's board. You will print the week's revenue, post the Actual, Frozen, Delta, and COGS sheets to their wall sleeves, and set up next week's Frozen print for the green clipboard.
1

Go to the Optimizer for the current month.

2

Go to the Dunham's Rev tab.

3

Select all revenue cells for the current week.

4

Print on Letter-size landscape paper, set to Selected Cells Only.

5

With a marker, write "Actual" and the total revenue for this week in large letters across the bottom of the sheet.

6

Place it in the sleeve labeled ACTUAL at the top of the wall.

7

Take the "Frozen" print-out of last week from the GREEN clipboard under the Frozen Attendance sign on the wall.

8

Place it in the sleeve marked FROZEN.

9

Print next week the same way you printed Actual (steps 3–4). With a marker, label the bottom "Frozen" and write the revenue total in large letters.

10

Put it on the GREEN clipboard for use the following week.

11

On a blank sheet of letter paper, write % Performance in large letters — Actual revenue ÷ Frozen as a percentage. (Example: 95%)

12

Place it in the sleeve marked DELTA.

13

On a blank sheet of letter paper, write in large letters the COGS percentage from the bottom-right of the current week on the Dunham's Rev tab in the Optimizer.

14

Place the sheet in the sleeve labeled COGS.

Bottom line: Print this week = Actual, last week's Frozen goes up, print next week's Frozen for the clipboard, then post Delta (%) and COGS (%). SOP 3.2 — End-of-Week Top Line
