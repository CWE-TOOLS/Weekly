The month revolves around two plan meetings — a 1st-half and a 2nd-half plan meeting. Ahead of each, prepare the Sales Quotes for that half's castings: one quote per casting, all its material (Mill → Shipping), dated Ordered-on = the 1st of the month.

Before the order ships, reconcile the quotes one final time against the month's Releasability report — the list must match exactly, nothing extra or missing. Then generate the Quotes report and email it to the Director of Operations and Accounting by the second-half plan meeting.

Verify the coming week is fully scheduled and print the Phase Start report, then create one Inflow Sales Order per casting — all the material for that week's work, dated the following Monday.

Run those orders into pic lists — a Facilities master plus a list per department Team Leader — and rack them under the "Staging Team Leaders Next Day" sign. Finally, email the week's Actual Material Usage to Ops and Accounting to close the loop back to planning.
