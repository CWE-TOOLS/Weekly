Standard Operating Procedure Creating an Optimizer Plan

Rev: 0.1 (draft)
Cadence: Per project
Owner: ____________
Estimate the labor manhours for every casting, task by task, in the Project Portal's Optimizer Hours tab. These estimates feed the schedule and become final after the Classroom review.
1

Open the Project Portal and open the project. A new project must be saved before Optimizer Hours can be used.

2

Make sure all castings have been added in the Castings tab — the Optimizer estimates hours per casting, so there is nothing to fill out until they exist.

3

Click the Optimizer Hours tab. On first open, every casting is seeded with the default task list:

MillForm OutCastDMG/FGOFTURackStripSealBuild CrateLoad CrateShip

4

Click a casting's pill at the top (or use the ‹ › arrows) to bring it into view — the workspace shows three castings side by side.

5

Fit the task list to the casting's work: click + Add Task for anything extra (type the name, press Enter), × to delete a task that doesn't apply, and drag the grip handle to reorder.

6

Enter the manhours for each task in its hrs field — whole numbers only. Everything saves automatically; there is no Save button.

7

Record any assumptions: Add description on a task card for task notes, or the Notes box at the top of the tab for project-wide notes.

8

For similar castings, use the copy and paste icons on the column headers to copy one casting's full task list (names, hours, notes) onto another, then adjust. Paste replaces the target casting's list.

9

Repeat for every casting in the project, and sanity-check each casting's Total and the Grand Total at the bottom.

10

To print, click Print Optimizer, select the castings, and click Print — one casting per page with its task/hours table.

11

Review the hours at the Classroom meetings and modify them per feedback from the Team Leaders.

12

Once confirmed at Classroom 3, mark Final Optimizer HRS complete for that casting on the Releasability Board — the hours are now the final production estimate.

Bottom line: Add castings, open Optimizer Hours, fit the task list, enter whole-number manhours per task per casting (copy/paste between similar casts), review with Team Leaders at Classroom, then mark Final Optimizer HRS on the Releasability Board. Creating an Optimizer Plan 5.1
