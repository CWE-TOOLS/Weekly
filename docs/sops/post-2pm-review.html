Standard Operating Procedure Post 2:00 PM Review

Rev: 1.0
Cadence: Daily — after 2:00 PM
Owner: ____________
After the 2:00 PM review, record the day's actual revenue, create the following week's Frozen Weekly target, and log the day's QA results.
1

Wait for all seniors to complete their end-of-day paperwork and place it in the black bin.

2

Take the Frozen daily from the wall under the black bin.

3

Fill out the actual revenue for each department and the total for the day.

4

Fill out any wins or losses for the day at the bottom of the Frozen daily.

5

Pin it to the wall under the green clipboard.

6

Create the Frozen Weekly for the following week — go to the Optimizer for the current month.

7

Go to the Dunham's Rev tab.

8

Select all cells for the following week.

9

Print, set to Print Selected Cells Only.

10

With a marker, write "Frozen" on the bottom in large letters with the revenue total.

11

Pin it to the wall under the black bin, labeled Frozen.

12

Grab the weekly QA Summary clipboard.

13

For each QA that day, put a check in that department's box for the day of the week — red check = Fail  green check = Pass. One check per project QA.

14

Fill out the log at the bottom of the QA sheet for any failed inspection — list an explanation for each failed QA to be reviewed Monday.

15

Return the QA clipboard to the wall.

16

Create the Daily Frozen for the following business day — open the Weekly Schedule.

17

Click the printer icon in the top-right corner.

18

Select Frozen Daily.

19

Select all departments.

20

Print the Frozen Daily and pin it up under the "Frozen Daily" sign.

Bottom line: Record actual revenue on the Frozen daily, create & label the following week's Frozen Weekly, then log each department's QA pass/fail and explain any fails for Monday. Post 2:00 PM Review
