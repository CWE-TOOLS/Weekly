Standard Operating Procedure Generate Pic Lists

Rev: 1.0
Cadence: Weekly
Owner: ____________
After the weekly Sales Orders are done, generate the material pic lists from Inflow for the following week — the Facilities master report plus a list per department Team Leader — and place them for staging.
1

Complete and verify all Sales Orders per the Generate Weekly Project Sales Orders SOP.

2

Open Inflow.

3

Hover over Reports and click Sales in the left sidebar.

4

Click Product Sales.

5

Click Details, then click Select.

6

Click Status and check Unfulfilled, Started, and Fulfilled only; click Apply.

7

Click the Date filter, then Custom; set the start and end to the following Monday and click Apply.

8

Click Generate in the center of the screen.

9

Print this report, staple it, and label it "Facilities" + the week (ex. June Week 1).

This is the master report Facilities uses to distribute all material the following week and stage material on Friday.

10

Now generate a pic list for each Team Leader — with the same Status and date settings, click the Category filter and print a report for each of the 4 departments. Label each with the week and department.

Casting / Demold Crating / Special Finishing / Sealing Formout / Mill

11

Also print several copies of these lists and staple them to any department that would receive items on them:

Non-Stocked Tool / Equip / Maint / Safety

12

Once all pic lists are printed, place the Team Leader lists in the metal rack under the "Staging Team Leaders Next Day" sign near the door to the production office.

Bottom line: With Sales Orders verified, run the Product Sales report (Unfulfilled/Started/Fulfilled, dated next Monday): print the Facilities master, a list per department, and the Non-Stocked / Tool lists — then rack the Team Leader lists for staging. Generate Pic Lists
