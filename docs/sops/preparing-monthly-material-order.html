Standard Operating Procedure Preparing Monthly Material Order

Rev: 1.0
Cadence: Monthly
Owner: ____________
Reconcile Inflow Sales Quotes against the month's Releasability report so the order covers all — and only — the castings running that month, then generate the report and email it to the Director of Operations and Accounting by the second-half plan meeting.
1

Open the Releasability Board.

2

Click the printer icon in the top-right corner.

3

Make sure Releasability Report is selected.

4

Check the box for the month you are creating the order for. Use the dropdown to select specific weeks if required.

5

Print the report for your reference going forward.

6

Open Inflow.

7

Click the Sales icon on the left sidebar, then click Sales Quotes.

8

Click Order Date, then click Custom.

9

Set both the start and end of the range to the first of the month you are creating the order for.

10

Click Apply.

11

Ensure all — and only — the castings from the Releasability report you printed are in the list of quotes.

12

If a quote is missing, find or create it and set its Ordered on date to the first of the order month.

13

If a quote is not on the Releasability list, move its Ordered on date to the first of a future or past month, based on where it belongs.

14

Verify all Sales Quotes for the month match the Releasability list — nothing extra, nothing missing.

15

To generate the order report, click Report on the left sidebar, then click Details and Select.

16

Under Status, filter for Quotes only; set the date range start and end to the 1st of the order month.

17

After applying both the Status and date filters, PDF-print the report.

18

Email the report to the Director of Operations and Accounting by the second-half plan meeting.

Bottom line: Print the month's Releasability report, filter Inflow quotes to the 1st of the month, make the quote list match it exactly (Ordered-on = 1st), then PDF the report and email Ops + Accounting by the second-half plan meeting. Monthly Material Order 4.2
