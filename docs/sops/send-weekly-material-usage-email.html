Standard Operating Procedure Send Weekly Material Usage Email

Rev: 1.0
Cadence: Weekly
Owner: ____________
Pull the week's Actual Material Usage report from Inflow, save it as a PDF, and email it to the Director of Operations and Accounting.
1

Open Inflow.

2

Click Reports on the left sidebar.

3

Click Sales on the left.

4

Click Product Sale on the right.

5

Switch from Summary to Details, then click Select.

6

Click Status and check Unfulfilled, Started, and Fulfilled only.

7

Click Apply.

8

Click the Date Range to the right of that.

9

Set both the beginning and end of the range to the current week's Monday.

10

Click Apply.

11

Click Generate.

12

Click Print in the top-right.

13

Print to PDF and name the file like this example: 6 29 26 Actual Material Usage

Use the current week's Monday date (M D YY).

14

Attach the PDF and email it to the Director of Operations and Accounting.

Bottom line: Inflow → Report → Sales → Product Sale (Details); Status = Unfulfilled/Started/Fulfilled; date range = this week's Monday; Generate → Print to PDF; email to the Director of Operations and Accounting. Weekly Material Usage Email 4.5
